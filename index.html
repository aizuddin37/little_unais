Welcome to

Discover the enriching journey of early education with Malaysia Sunnah Preschool of Little Unais, where nurturing young minds through a blend of modern education and cherished traditional values creates a foundation for a bright future.

Why choose LITTLE UNAIS?

At Little Unais, we foster Islamic values into children daily routines. We are dedicated to provide a holistic approach of learning that embraces intellectual and spiritual growth including emotional, social, and physical development. Our team of experienced educators and caregivers are committed to create a nurturing environment that mirrors the warmth of home.

Holistic Islamic Education & Integration of Islamic Values

We provide a comprehensive early Islamic education that encompasses not only academic learning but also moral and ethical learning which aligns with Islamic principles.

Well-Experienced, Passionate and Loving Educators

Our dedicated teachers who are not only experts in early childhood education but also have a deep understanding of Islamic teachings. They create a warm and supportive environment where they recognize and nurture each child's individual needs and talents

Focus on Character Development

At our preschool, we emphasize character development by teaching good moral values to the children and encouraging them to practice these values in their daily interactions.

Safe and Inclusive Environment

We prioritize the safety and well-being of every child. Our preschool is a secure space where children can explore, learn, and play freely. We promote inclusivity and acceptance, ensuring that every child feels valued and respected regardless of their background.

Parental Involvement

We encourage active parental involvement, providing opportunities for parents to participate in school activities, workshops, and events. This collaboration ensures a consistent and supportive learning environment for the child both at home and at school.

Preparation for a Bright Future

Our esteemed Islamic preschool aim to prepare children academically while equips them with strong moral values, leadership skills, and a sense of responsibility. Little Unais graduates are paving the way for a bright and fulfilling future, In Shaa Allah!
